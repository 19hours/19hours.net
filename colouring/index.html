Colouring

Colouring brings me back to when I was a kid. I went for crayon lessons but my art teacher was not very creative.

So now I am colouring pictures and writing haikus every weekend. Sometimes the haikus come together with the drawings but sometimes I don't know. I will upload colour pencil art here (poems can be found here). It's really fun so you should try it too. I hope you enjoy the pictures as much as I do.
